01The Problem

Printed QR codes should not become obsolete.

A standard QR code is static. The destination is encoded directly into the pattern, so the moment it is printed, the link is locked forever.

When that link changes — a campaign ends, a menu updates, a domain moves — the printed material stops working. The business card, the label, the packaging, the poster all become dead weight.

The QR code outlives the link it was built to carry.

02How It Works

Relinq separates the QR from the destination.

  1. 01Step

    Print one permanent Relinq QR

    Generate a single QR code tied to your account. Its pattern never changes — print it on anything, once.

  2. 02Step

    Manage the destination in your account

    The QR points to a Relinq address. Behind it, you set the real link your audience should reach.

  3. 03Step

    Change the link anytime

    Update the destination from your dashboard. Every existing scan instantly routes to the new target.
